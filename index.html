Welcome to Aruba

Bon Bini
Summer Camps!

This summer is about showing up with care, energy, and openness. Together with CEDE Aruba, the EUHUB, BARiO UNICO, and the participating neighbourhood centres, your task will be to help create safe, joyful, and meaningful camp days for children across Aruba.

Open Agenda Useful Information

You help make the summer happen.

By showing up with kindness, creativity, and commitment, you become part of something bigger than yourself. Your role helps create fun, connection, and impact across the whole programme.

What you take home matters too.

This summer is not only about giving — it is also about learning, growing, and discovering what you are capable of. You will gain experience, connection, and a stronger sense of purpose.

What are your main tasks:

01

Lead Fun Activities

Plan and lead games, workshops, and creative activities for children.

02

Support Camp Programs

Help with sports, cultural, and educational activities throughout the camp.

03

Work as a Team

Support local staff in the daily coordination and organization of camp life.

04

Bring Your Energy

Share enthusiasm, fresh ideas, and an international spirit to create a memorable summer experience.

05

Experience is a Plus

Previous experience working with children is an advantage, but not required.
